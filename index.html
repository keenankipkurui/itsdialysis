About Us

Solutions in Kidney care

The burden of Chronic Kidney Failure has been increasing around the globe and affects approximately 10% of the adult population. Chronic Renal Disease is caused by several illnesses including diabetes, hypertension, vascular disease, and glomerulonephritis. Individuals with this condition have no option but to seek dialysis services at various health facilities. In Africa, the dialysis centers are few hence people must traverse long distances to seek these interventions.

Our Services

Hemodialysis

Hemodialysis is a treatment to filter wastes and water from your blood, as your kidneys did when they were healthy. Hemodialysis helps control blood pressure and balance important minerals, such as potassium, sodium, and calcium, in your blood.

Peritoneal dialysis

is a way to remove waste products from your blood when your kidneys can't adequately do the job any longer. This procedure filters the blood in a different way than does the more common blood-filtering procedure called hemodialysis

Kidney transplant

is a surgical procedure to place a healthy kidney from a living or deceased donor into a person whose kidneys no longer function properly.
